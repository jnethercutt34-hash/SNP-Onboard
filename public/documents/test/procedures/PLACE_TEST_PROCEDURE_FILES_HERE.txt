Test Procedures
================
Step-by-step procedures executed during testing. Traceable to test plans.

Naming: SNP-PROC-<TYPE>-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-PROC-ATP-001_RevB.pdf        — Acceptance Test Procedure
  SNP-PROC-VIBRATION-002_RevA.pdf  — Vibration test procedure
  SNP-PROC-THERMAL-003_RevA.pdf    — Thermal vacuum test procedure
  SNP-PROC-FWUPD-004_RevA.pdf      — Firmware update procedure
  SNP-PROC-CRYPTO-005_RevA.pdf     — Crypto unit key-loading procedure
