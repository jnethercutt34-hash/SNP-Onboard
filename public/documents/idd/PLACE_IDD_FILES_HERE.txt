Interface Design Documents (IDD)
=================================
Detailed design specifications for each interface defined in the ICDs.

Naming: SNP-IDD-<INTERFACE>-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-IDD-SERIAL-001_RevA.pdf      — Serial management bus design
  SNP-IDD-OPTICAL-002_RevA.pdf     — 10G optical interface design
  SNP-IDD-CRYPTO-003_RevB.pdf      — Crypto unit utility plane interface design
