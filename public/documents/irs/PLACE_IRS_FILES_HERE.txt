Interface Requirements Specifications (IRS)
============================================
Interface requirements captured before design begins. Parent to the IDD.

Naming: SNP-IRS-<INTERFACE>-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-IRS-EXTERNAL-001_RevA.pdf    — External system interface requirements
  SNP-IRS-INTERNAL-002_RevA.pdf    — Internal VPX bus interface requirements
