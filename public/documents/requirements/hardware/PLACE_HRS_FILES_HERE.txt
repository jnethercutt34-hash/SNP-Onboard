Hardware Requirements Specification (HRS)
==========================================
Hardware-level requirements derived from the SyRS. Covers GPP cards,
mezzanines, PSUs, and expansion modules.

Naming: SNP-HRS-<SUBSYSTEM>-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-HRS-GPP-001_RevB.pdf         — GPP card hardware requirements
  SNP-HRS-PSU-002_RevA.pdf         — Power converter requirements
  SNP-HRS-MEZ-003_RevA.pdf         — Mezzanine hardware requirements
  SNP-HRS-CHASSIS-004_RevA.pdf     — Chassis & mechanical requirements
