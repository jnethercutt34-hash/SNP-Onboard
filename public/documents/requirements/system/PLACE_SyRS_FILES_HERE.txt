System Requirements Specification (SyRS)
==========================================
Top-level system requirements for the SNP platform. Allocated to hardware,
software, and firmware subsystems.

Naming: SNP-SyRS-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-SyRS-001_RevC.pdf            — SNP System Requirements Specification
  SNP-SyRS-CONOPS-002_RevA.pdf     — Concept of Operations document
