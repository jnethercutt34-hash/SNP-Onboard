Firmware Design Documents (FDD)
================================
FPGA bitstream design, BSP device tree, bootloader configuration,
and chassis management firmware.

Naming: SNP-FDD-<MODULE>-<NNN>_Rev<X>.pdf

Examples:
  SNP-FDD-FPGA-001_RevB.pdf        — FPGA design document (FFT/FEC cores)
  SNP-FDD-FPGA-PR-002_RevA.pdf     — Partial reconfiguration design
  SNP-FDD-BSP-003_RevB.pdf         — Linux BSP & device tree design
  SNP-FDD-CMC-004_RevA.pdf         — UT32M0R500 chassis management firmware
  SNP-FDD-UBOOT-005_RevA.pdf       — U-Boot bootloader configuration
